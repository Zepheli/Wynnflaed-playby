Wynnflaed

A thin girl with pale complexion, Wynnflaed stands a bit under the mean Felinae female height, but sports an otherwise average build for her kind, with soft skin and prominent ears and tail. Her hair, long and dark, is usually tied in a simple ponytail and her body language suggests a degree of impatience balanced by mature deference. Her accent is moderately foreign, and she pronounces her own name 'WOON-flad.'

Under her robes, she makes a point of wearing no sleeves, revealing the mark of Folhammar on her right bicep.

The small beastkin has given up much of herself to adapt to the cruel climate and demanding society of Vdalion, and while she would never regret joining her new Folhammar family, the war with Delphinia has torn apart her pure concept of morality.
